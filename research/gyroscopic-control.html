Gyroscopic Control of Robotic Smart Vehicles Using SO3

ASME-IMECE2023 Conference Paper

Project Overview

This research focuses on the application of gyroscopic control systems in robotic smart vehicles using SO3 mathematics for improved stability and maneuverability.

Key Contributions

  • Developed novel gyroscopic control algorithms
  • Implemented SO3 mathematical framework
  • Conducted extensive simulation and testing
  • Presented findings at ASME-IMECE2023
