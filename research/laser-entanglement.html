Laser-induced Entanglement of Liquid Metal and Metallic Nanowire

European Materials Research Society Conference 2022

Project Overview

Research on laser-induced entanglement techniques for creating monolithically variable stretchable conductors using liquid metal and metallic nanowires.

Conference Presentation

Presented at The European Materials Research Society at Warsaw University of Technology 2022

Key Research Areas

  • Laser-induced entanglement processes
  • Liquid metal manipulation
  • Metallic nanowire integration
  • Stretchable conductor development

Applications

Monolithically variable stretchable conductors for flexible electronics and wearable technology
