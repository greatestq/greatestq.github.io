Space Dynamics Tutor

August 2022 - May 2023

Course Overview

Tutoring students in Space Dynamics, covering orbital mechanics, spacecraft dynamics, and control systems for space applications.

Course Topics

  • Orbital mechanics and Kepler's laws
  • Two-body and three-body problems
  • Orbital maneuvers and transfers
  • Spacecraft attitude dynamics
  • Attitude control systems
  • Space environment effects
  • Mission planning and analysis
  • Numerical methods in orbital mechanics

Teaching Methods

  • Mathematical derivations and proofs
  • Computer simulations and modeling
  • Case studies of real missions
  • Problem-solving workshops
  • Software tools (MATLAB, STK)

Practical Applications

Connected theoretical concepts to real-world space missions, satellite operations, and aerospace engineering applications.
