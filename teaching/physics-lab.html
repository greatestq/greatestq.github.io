August 2022 - May 2023

Course Overview

Teaching Assistant for Physics Laboratory courses, providing hands-on instruction and guidance to students in experimental physics.

Responsibilities

  • Led laboratory sessions and demonstrations
  • Provided one-on-one guidance to students
  • Graded laboratory reports and assignments
  • Assisted in experiment setup and safety protocols
  • Conducted review sessions and office hours

Course Topics

  • Mechanics and motion
  • Waves and oscillations
  • Electricity and magnetism
  • Optics and light
  • Data analysis and error propagation

Teaching Approach

Emphasized hands-on learning, critical thinking, and proper experimental methodology while ensuring student safety and engagement.
