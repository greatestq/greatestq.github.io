  • Applications in engineering

  • Problem-solving strategies
  • Geometric interpretations
  • Computational methods
  • Real-world applications

Student Outcomes

Helped students develop strong analytical skills and mathematical intuition for solving complex engineering problems using linear algebra techniques.
